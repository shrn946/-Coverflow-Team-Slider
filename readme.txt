The Coverflow Team Slider for WordPress is a plugin designed to enhance your website with an interactive team slider feature. With just a simple shortcode [team_slider], you can effortlessly integrate a dynamic slider showcasing your team members.

Features
Easy integration using the [team_slider] shortcode.
Adds an attractive Coverflow design to your team slider.
Showcase your team members in an interactive and engaging manner.
Enhance the visual appeal of your website with smooth slider transitions.

Usage
To add the team slider to your website, simply insert the [team_slider] shortcode into any post, page, or widget where you want it to appear.
[team_slider]
